Gameplay

In this step, we will install mods aiming to improve the general gameplay

It is especially important to read the descriptions of these mods.

Creating a Separator in MO2.

  1. Right-click the empty space in the left pane of MO2 and select Create separator.
  2. Name the separator Gameplay.

Loot Logic and Reduction With optional Harvest Restrictions

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Loot Logic and Reduction 1.5.3.1
  • Loot Logic and Reduction - CFM Patch
Reduces ammo, meds and bobby pin drops while logically diversifying loot into appropriate containers.

Complex Vendors

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Complex Vendors
Makes most vendors only buy items related to what they sell (for example doctors will only buy medical supplies).

Blocking Overhaul

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Blocking Overhaul
Revamps the blocking system.

Chemfluence - AI Combat Dynamics

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Chemfluence AI Combat Dynamics
Enhances AI behavior by integrating two key factors: chem usage and weapon choice. NPCs will not only respond to chem consumption with distinct combat strategies, but also adapt their tactics based on the weapons they wield.

SPARS - Simple Power Armor Reduction System

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - SPARS-50 Main File
Reduces the frequency of power armors.

DLC Timing

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - DLC Timing
  • DLC Timing - UFO4P Patch
Allows you to change the starting conditions for Automatron, Far Harbor, Nuka World and Vault-Tec Workshop independently to suit your playthrough.

Simple Creation Club Delayed NG

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Simple Creation Club Delayed NG
Delays the start of the Next Gen Creation Club content until certain requirements are met.

Keep Radiants in the Commonwealth UFO4P Version

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Keep Radiants in the Commonwealth UFO4P Version
Prevents radiant quests from sending you outside the Commonwealth.

Encounter Zone Recalculation (Continuous Level Scaling)

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Encounter Zone Recalculation 1.0 (Click the blue name above for a direct link to the correct version)
Allows previously-visited encounter zones to update the levels of the enemies in them as the player levels up.

Who's The General - Minutemen Quest Cleanup

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Who's The General - Minutemen Quest Cleanup
  • Optional Files - 1 - Keep Radiants in the Commonwealth Patch
Does multiple things:
  • Most Minutemen quests have been moved to Preston's "How are you doing?" dialogue option.
  • Preston Garvey no longer gives you a new quest as part of turning in an old quest.
  • As General of the Minutemen, you no longer report back to Preston at the end of most quests.
  • Radio Freedom now broadcasts even when the player isn't tuned in, fixing radios tuned to the station.
  • The player will only acquire quests from Radio Freedom while tuned in on their Pip-Boy, or listening at The Castle.

Molotov Cocktail Nerf

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Molotov Cocktail Nerf
Nerfs the molotov's insane damage on hit and removes its stagger.

Legendaries They Can Use

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Legendaries They Can Use
Scans the NPCs inventory and picks a random item they already have to slap a legendary on. The item will then be used in their attempt to kill you.

No Sneaking in Power Armor

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - No Sneaking in Power Armor
Sneaking is now far, far harder in power armor.

Hit Those Objects

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Hit Those Objects
Adds contextual hit reactivity to various objects and items in the world.

Hit Those Explosives

Installation instructions:

  • Main Files - Hit Those Explosives
Hitting explosive weapons and ammo will now cause them to explode, allowing for chain reactions.
